11 oz strong flour
3/4 cup water
1 tsp active dry yeast
3/4 tsp kosher salt

Sprinkle yeast in water, let sit to dissolve 5 min. Add flour, sprinkle
salt.  Mix well, then knead (smear, stretch, tear) about 5 min until
windowpane forms.  Let rise two hours until tripled. Divide into six,
dip in flour, roll or push or pull into 5-6 inch rounds. Fry in a
little oil in cast iron pan preheated on med-high one min, turn for
another min. Sprinkle with kosher salt. Best served warm.
