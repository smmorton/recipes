11oz strong flour
3/4 C water
1 t active dry yeast
3/4 t kosher salt

Sprinkle yeast in water, let sit to disolve 5 min. Add flour, sprinkle
salt.  Mix well, then need (smear, stretch, tear) 5 min or until
windowpane forms.  Let rise two hours until tripled. Divide into six,
dip in flour, roll or push or pull into 5-6 inch rounds. Fry in a
little oil in cast iron pan prehetaed on med hi one min, turn for
another min. Sprinkle with kosher salt.
